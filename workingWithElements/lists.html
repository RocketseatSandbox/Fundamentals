The detox Juice

Ingredients:

  • 3 leafs of green cabbage
  • 1 orange
  • 1 little piece of ginger
  • 300ml of water
  • Sweetener at will
  • Ice at will

Steps:

  1. Take all the ingredients, except ice, into a blender
  2. Divide the juice on cups, after put the ice
